About Us

Jo World Agencies

Innovate, Transform & Empower

With over 22+ years’ experience as an infrastructure operator, technology integrator and supplier of value-added services,

Jo World Agencies supports business, government and public sector organizations through every step of their digital transformation. Data is at the heart of digital transformation for businesses. Our products and Partnerships have served numerous customers across the continent. Our customers include some of the large private and public corporations as well as some of the fastest growing early stage companies. Jo World Agencies is a leading performer in the “data journey,” offering its customers exceptional expertise in the collection, transfer, security, storage, processing, analysis, sharing of data and value creation.

We're fortunate to work with fantastic clients from across the region, partnering with global market leaders to provide excellent ICT solutions.
